Cost of Doing Nothing — Calculator
Revenue lever Customer-facing Pre-sales

  • Champions get a number they own, not a vendor claim
Became an outbounding tool. Attached a real cost to not upgrading to Enterprise dbt. Gave AEs a concrete anchor where there wasn't one.

Team multiplier Customer-facing Enablement

Other SAs started doing the same. Using Claude to build architecture diagrams for their own deals. The concept spread because I demonstrated it and left behind a system for replicating it.

Became a shared team pattern. SAs now build custom diagrams for their own deals. Quality of technical communication went up without me in the room.
Workshop-Driven "Soft POC" Motion
Deal execution Strategic
  • Workshop format replacing formal POC overhead

  • Eliminated formal POC in several deals
  • Now part of how I ramp AEs
